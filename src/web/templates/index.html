Get instant Discord notifications every time a new order arrives in your Etsy shop.

Add to Discord

How it works

1

Click Add to Discord and choose your server.

2

Shopkeep will DM you a link to connect your Etsy shop.

3

Use /setchannel in Discord to pick where notifications go.

4

That's it — new orders appear automatically, no refreshing required.
