About

Built for Etsy sellers

A small tool that solves a specific problem.

Shopkeep started as a simple utility for Etsy sellers who wanted real-time order alerts without constantly checking their phone or email. Discord is already where many small businesses gather with their teams, so connecting the two felt natural.

Simple setup

Add the bot to your server, authorize your Etsy account through a secure OAuth flow, and pick the channel where you want notifications. After that, Shopkeep runs in the background.

Privacy first

Shopkeep uses the official Etsy API v3. It requests only the permissions it needs: read access to your transactions, listings, and shop profile. It never writes to your Etsy account and never shares your data.

Built with
Python discord.py Etsy API v3 Flask aiosqlite
